Foundational principles and visual identity

Design Tokens
A collection of design decisions stored in key-value pairs, abstracted so they can easily be used across a wide range of platforms, design and code.
Accessibility
Prioritizing accessibility in our design system allows all users, regardless of ability, to navigate and interact with our products.
Web Components
Reusable front-end components shared across codebases, with built-in design, state, and behavior.
Typography
A collection of standard NYS brand-approved typefaces that need to be used in both design and development product work.
