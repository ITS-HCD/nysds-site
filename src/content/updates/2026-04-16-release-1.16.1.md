## `<nys-datepicker>` minDate and maxDate support
We've added the ability to restrict selectable dates in the datepicker component. See it live in action below. Special thanks to [@Sqrrl](https://github.com/sqrrl) for maintaining the base wc-datepicker component our nys-datepicker is based off of.
[Read the minDate maxDate documentation](/components/datepicker/#restrict-selectable-dates).

{% set preview %}
<nys-datepicker
  label="Select a date"
  description="Only dates within April 4/5/2026 - 4/15/2026 are selectable"
  minDate="2026-04-05"
  maxDate="2026-04-15"
></nys-datepicker>
