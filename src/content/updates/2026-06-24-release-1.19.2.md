## 🚨 Breaking changes

- `<nys-icon>` in 1.19.0 libraries break on "chunked" frameworks (like React, Angular). [Library break fixed in 1.19.1](/components/icon/#override-the-default-library)
- `nys-grid-gap` in 1.19.0 added vertical spacing to `grid-gap` gutters. [Read about the grid-gap classes](/foundations/utilities/grid/#gutters)
- `<nys-datepicker>` default `width` changed from `md` to `full`. [See note about width](components/datepicker/#width)
