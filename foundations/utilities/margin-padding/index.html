Margin and Padding

Margin

Margin appears as spacing outside the container it is applied to.

.nys-margin-1px
.nys-margin-2px
.nys-margin-50
.nys-margin-100
.nys-margin-150
.nys-margin-200
.nys-margin-250
.nys-margin-300
.nys-margin-400
.nys-margin-500
.nys-margin-600
.nys-margin-700
.nys-margin-800
.nys-margin-1200

You can also apply margin in vertical, horizontal, and individual directions.
.nys-margin-x-800 .nys-margin-y-200
.nys-margin-t-50
.nys-margin-r-200
.nys-margin-b-400
.nys-margin-l-800
